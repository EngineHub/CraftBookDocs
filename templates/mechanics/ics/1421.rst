==============
Clock [MC1421]
==============

**Clocks**, are an ic that allows precise timing. So, for example, if you specify the integer on the sign to be 15 it will take
15 redstone ticks and if 

==============
Sign parameters
===============
   1. Leave first line blank

   2. Type on 2nd line: [MC1421] 

   3. And on the third line type the amount of 2 ticks the clock should take for it to give an output! 
   
Side-note: Do **[MC1421]S** to make it Self-triggered and give it a redstone signal to stop it

Please note that behind the block with the sign should be a lever connected to the redstone
