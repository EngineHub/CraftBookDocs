**MC1421** is an IC that allows for a precisely timed redstone signal.
The IC toggles between rising and falling states every X amount of ticks.

Sign parameters
===============

  1. Blank
  2. [MC1421] or [MC1421]S
  3. Amount of redstone ticks between toggle
  4. Blank
